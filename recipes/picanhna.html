Lasagna

Description

Picanhna is a south american delight. it pleases your senses and the beef fat melts in your mouth literally.

Ingredients:

  • picanha beef
  • salt & pepper
  • chimichurri
  • french fries

Steps:

  1. Sear the beef
  2. Cook in the oven
  3. Salt pepper to taste
  4. Add salt pepper
